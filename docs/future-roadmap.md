# StackSphere Future Roadmap

This document captures upcoming ideas and future expansions for StackSphere post-launch.

---

## 📅 Roadmap Milestones

### Phase 1: Real-Time Chat System
* Integrate WebSockets (Socket.io or Ably) to let users direct-message (DM) friends or form group channels.

### Phase 2: AI-Powered Assistance
* Add an AI module to automatically analyze questions, detect duplicate entries, and suggest solutions/resources from StackSphere archives before users submit posts.

### Phase 3: Developer Portfolio Analytics
* Provide developers with advanced data analytics panels detailing:
  * Views/read-time of their questions and answers.
  * Point earning trends.
  * Profile activity chart (similar to GitHub's contribution matrix).

### Phase 4: Push Notification Hub
* Build in-app and browser push notifications for:
  * New answers on their posted questions.
  * Friend requests or social mentions.
  * Reward achievements.
